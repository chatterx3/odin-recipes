Pumpkin Pie

Description

This is delicious pumpkin pie. We will be using canned pumpkin, but homemade whipped cream!

Ingredients

  • 32oz Libby's Pumpkin Pie Mix
  • 3/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 Tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Mix together the pumpkin pie mix and evaporated milk.
  2. Pour into pie pan
  3. Cook 50 minutes
  4. Cool 2 hours
  5. Pour heavy cream into iced bowl and whip into soft peak form
